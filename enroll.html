Registration Rules

  1. Attendance: Maintaining regular attendance is crucial to retaining your eligibility to complete the courses.
  2. Roadmap Updates: The course roadmap may be updated at any time to reflect the latest trends and demands in the local industry.
  3. Course Format: All main courses are conducted online. On-site practice labs are exclusively available at Al-Hussein Bin Talal University in Ma'an.
  4. Progression Requirements: You must complete all tasks, projects, and occasionally exams or interviews to advance to the next level.
  5. Voluntary Camp: This bootcamp is a volunteer initiative by the Syntax Jo team and is entirely free of charge.
  6. Enrollment Information: Ensure that you provide accurate information in the enrollment form. We will contact you via email and WhatsApp.
